PyNash is a Nashville and Middle Tennessee community of people who use Python for business or pleasure. We welcome people of all experience and skill levels.

You can see us in person when we meet on the 4th Thursday of the month for night-time meetings, and on the 1st Wendnesday of the month for lunches. If you want to chat with us in between meetings, join us in the #python channel in the NashDev Slack.

  • Find us on:
  • Meetup
  • Twitter
  • Github
  • Slack

Our Next Meeting

Want to speak?

If you have a talk you'd like to give, or maybe you just want to give public speaking a try, use our speaker sign-up form to sign up for your PyNash talk!

If you've never given a talk before and want a mentor or feedback on your talk, please get in touch and we'll find someone to help you.

Looking for a job?

If you're new to town or fresh out of school and looking for a job, you can check out the NashDev Jobs Board to see who's hiring.

If you're an employer who has a job opening and would like to reach the local community, you should consider posting your your job to the NashDev Jobs Board.

Want to sponsor?

Are you hiring? Want to support the local community? Have a product you think our members would like? A PyNash sponsorship is exactly what you need.

Check out our sponorship prospectus and get in touch if you'd like to sponsor a meetup.

Code of Conduct

Attendees of every PyNash meeting are expected to behave in accordance with our Code of Conduct. Please read the Code of Conduct and reach out to us with any questions or concerns.
